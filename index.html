Salman Hashmi

Electrical and Software Engineer

Currently undergoing an industry-curated robotics training as part of Udacity's inaugural cohort in the Robotics program. Also completing graduate coursework in Software Engineering at Harvard University DCE through distance education, having completed a B.Eng degree in Electrical Engineering in 2015 from McGill University in Montreal, Canada. Previously, worked on IoT hardware platforms in the Advanced Networking Research Lab at the McGill School of Computer Science, publishing a book in Springer Briefs series. While at McGill, completed 3 full-time and 2 part-time Co-op terms at Ericsson Canada as a software developer. Prior to that, involved at McGill Robotics for a year as an Electrical Engineer for the Fourth Annual NASA Lunabotics. Skilled in areas of robotics, software engineering, deep learning and data science. Leadership and project management experience from co-founding the McGill Flying Club. Can be reached at sah517@g.harvard.edu.
